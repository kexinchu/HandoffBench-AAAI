# Human spot-check record v1

Recorded: 2026-07-22

## Reported observation

A human project author reported manually spot-checking a subset of frozen cases
and finding them consistent with the intended task and label semantics.

## Evidentiary status

This is an **informal author spot-check**, not an independent human-validity
study. The number of reviewed cases, their task IDs, the selection rule, the
rubric-level decisions, and whether the reviewer was blinded to model outcomes
have not yet been recorded. Accordingly, this observation is not used in the
paper's quantitative claims and does not justify describing the gold labels as
human-validated or expert-validated.

The frozen dataset, labels, and confirmatory results remain unchanged.

## Information required to finalize a citable audit

1. Reviewer role and relevant expertise (identity may remain private during
   anonymous review).
2. Review date, number of cases, task IDs, and the outcome-independent sampling
   rule or fixed sample-manifest hash.
3. Whether the reviewer was blinded to candidate-model conditions and outcomes.
4. Per-case judgments for public-contract uniqueness, gold-state completeness,
   field correctness, epistemic status, provenance, criticality, scoped consent,
   and legal terminal action sequence.
5. Aggregate agreement/discrepancy counts and a statement that no frozen case
   was repaired, removed, or replaced after outcomes were observed.

Until those fields are supplied, the manuscript must retain its current
limitation that no recruited-human validation has been completed.
